Our Green Project: Reducing Costs and Environmental Impact

Our goal is to make shopping more sustainable by reducing the environmental impact of transportation. We aim to minimize the use of couriers and the carbon footprint of deliveries through an innovative system based on lockers—automated self-service stations for package pickup, similar to those used by Amazon, located across Italy.

These lockers are placed in convenient locations like shopping centers, public transport hubs, and residential areas, allowing customers to easily pick up their purchases without requiring home deliveries. This reduces the need for multiple courier trips, cutting down on fuel consumption and traffic congestion.

By using lockers, we not only create a more cost-effective delivery system but also help reduce emissions, contributing to a greener, more efficient shopping experience. This initiative makes it easier for everyone to adopt a more sustainable lifestyle, while saving both time and money.

With our green solution, we’re helping to make e-commerce more environmentally friendly, one delivery at a time.
